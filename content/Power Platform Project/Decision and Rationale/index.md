### **Decision and Rationale**

The **Decision and Rationale** section provides the underlying reasoning behind each major planning decision. It complements the formal project planning documents (e.g., Business Case, Project Charter) by offering transparent insight into the _why_ behind the _what_ — including choice of tools, methodologies, documentation structure, and phase definitions.

This section is designed to evolve alongside the project. As requirements shift or new priorities emerge, rationale entries will be refined to ensure traceability and clarity of thought throughout the project lifecycle.
