# Business Case – Health Tracking Portfolio App

## 1. Executive Summary
Create a Power Platform-based health tracking app as a portfolio piece to showcase technical and project management skills. Used to support job applications and personal wellness.

## 2. Problem / Opportunity Statement
There is no consolidated platform for documenting health metrics and behavioral trends, while also demonstrating the use of Power Platform tools and ALM practices for professional purposes.

## 3. Analysis of Options
- **Do Nothing:** No career benefit, no health tracking.
- **Use Off-the-Shelf App:** Doesn’t demonstrate technical skill or customization.
- ✅ **Build Custom App:** High flexibility, skill demonstration, reusable framework.

## 4. Strategic Alignment
- Supports professional growth and job search goals.
- Demonstrates Power Platform, Agile, and project planning expertise.

## 5. Expected Benefits
- Tangible: Job interview leverage, technical portfolio.
- Intangible: Improved health awareness, skill confidence.

## 6. Estimated Costs & Effort
- Time: ~60-100 hours over 3 phases.
- Tools: Power Platform dev time (free tenant), ClickUp, Obsidian, Cloudflare (free tiers).

## 7. Timeline & Phases
- **Phase 1** – App + Dataverse Setup (1 month)
- **Phase 2** – ALM & Pipelines (1 month)
- **Phase 3** – Power BI Integration (1 month)

## 8. Risks & Mitigations
- Risk: Scope creep ➤ Use ClickUp to limit sprint focus.
- Risk: Tech blockers ➤ Use phased implementation.

## 9. Recommendation
Proceed with Phase 1 development to establish core features and a portfolio-ready app.
